We make the world a brighter, happier place through the power of fragrance.

This idea is what we were founded on, and it’s at the heart of everything we do. We’re a team that cares about our customers and believes in giving them a reason to celebrate with fragrance every day.

We are committed to creating a diverse, equitable and inclusive culture that is focused on delivering exceptional fragrances and experiences. We work hard to improve our communities and our planet in a way that will make us proud for years to come ... because we believe the world is a better place when everyone has access to the things that make them happy. Bath & Body Works is an international company that sells personal care and beauty products in more than 1,700 North American stores and has nearly 300 stores in countries around the globe operating under franchise, license and wholesale arrangements.
